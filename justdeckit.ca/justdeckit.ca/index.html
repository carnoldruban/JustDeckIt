BUILDING DECKS SINCE 2005

Just Deck It (Deck Builder) specializes in the design and construction of custom decks. Serving the Greater Toronto Area in Ontario, Canada for over 10 years. We are a registered and insured business in the GTA. Just Deck It services The East End of Toronto and the Tri-County area of Hastings County including Belleville, Quinte West and Trenton. Northumberland County including Port Hope, Cobourg and Brighton. As well as, Prince Edward County including Picton, Wellington and Bloomfield.
Ryan Graziano is the owner and operator of Just Deck It. Our Team can help you design your dream custom deck or simply build from a plan you provide. He has had extensive experience building custom decks. With a variety of materials including, cedar, pressure treated lumber and composite pvc decking.
